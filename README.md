## 🚀 About Me

I'm a PhD candidate in Physics at the Federal University of Pelotas (UFPel), where I develop research at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence**, **Computational Materials Science**, and **High-Performance Computing (HPC)**.

My work focuses on applying **Machine Learning**, **Density Functional Theory (DFT)**, and **Molecular Simulations** to accelerate scientific discovery, especially in materials science and environmental applications.

Beyond academia, I enjoy building AI-powered applications, workflow automation tools, scientific software, and full-stack projects that solve real-world problems.
